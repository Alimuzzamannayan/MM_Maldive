## The Operational Reality of Running a Maldives Resort

Managing a resort in the Maldives is unlike any other hospitality operation in the world. Your property is on an island. Your staff live on-site. Supplies arrive by speedboat or seaplane. Your guests pay in USD, EUR, and CNY. And MIRA still expects your GST return on the 28th of every month.

Legacy systems — spreadsheets, standalone POS terminals, paper-based receiving logs — break down fast under this pressure. Odoo ERP was built for exactly this kind of distributed, multi-module operation.

## What Odoo Solves for Resorts

### Inventory and Procurement

Every item that comes onto your island — from laundry detergent to aged beef — needs to be tracked. Odoo's inventory module gives you:

- **Purchase orders** linked directly to supplier invoices
- **Reorder rules** that trigger purchase requisitions automatically when stock drops below threshold
- **Lot and serial number tracking** for high-value items and perishables
- **Landed cost allocation** — spread your speedboat delivery costs across the items in each shipment

### Accounting and MIRA Compliance

Odoo's accounting module is fully multi-currency and supports Maldivian GST natively. You can configure separate tax groups for the 16% tourism rate and the 6% general rate. Reports pull directly from transaction data, so your accountant can generate a GST return in minutes rather than days.

### Payroll and HR

Resorts typically operate with a mix of Maldivian nationals and work permit holders. Odoo HR handles:

- Multi-contract payroll with allowances (island allowance, food allowance, service charge)
- Work permit expiry alerts
- Leave management with island-specific approval flows

### Point of Sale Integration

Odoo POS integrates with your front office system so that bar tabs, spa treatments, and excursion bookings flow directly into guest folios and then into the accounting module. No manual data entry, no end-of-day reconciliation headaches.

## Multi-Property Management

If you operate more than one resort — or have a support office in Malé — Odoo's multi-company feature lets you manage all entities from a single platform. Intercompany transactions (Malé office supplying goods to the island, for example) are automated and visible across both books.

## The Migration Process

Switching to Odoo from a legacy system takes planning. Here is the typical approach we use for resort clients:

1. **Discovery** — map existing processes, data sources, and integrations
2. **Configuration** — set up chart of accounts, tax groups, product categories, and warehouse structure
3. **Data migration** — import supplier master, product catalogue, and opening balances
4. **Staff training** — on-site and remote sessions for operations, accounts, and F&B teams
5. **Go-live support** — dedicated support during the first month of live operation

Most resort implementations are complete within 8–12 weeks depending on property size and complexity.

## Is Odoo Right for Your Resort?

Odoo works best for properties with:
- More than 20 staff
- Regular procurement from multiple suppliers
- GST obligations (annual revenue over MVR 1,000,000)
- A need to reduce manual data entry and end-of-month scrambles

If you are running a small guesthouse with a single supplier and fewer than 10 staff, a lighter accounting tool may suit you better for now.
